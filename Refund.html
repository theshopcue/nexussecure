Refund & Return Policy

At NexusSecure, customer satisfaction is our highest priority. We understand the importance of transparency and clarity in every transaction. This Refund & Return Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are granted, ensuring a secure and smooth experience for all customers.

1. Digital Product Nature

All products offered through NexusSecure are digitally delivered antivirus software licenses. Once an order is confirmed and the license is electronically sent to the registered email address, the item is considered delivered and fulfilled.

Because of the digital format and the licensing terms associated with the software, returns are not applicable. However, we do offer refunds under specific circumstances outlined below.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

We are committed to resolving any issues that may arise during your purchase. A refund may be issued in the following cases:

  • Non-delivery of Product: If you have not received your product within 24 hours of payment confirmation and have contacted us without resolution.
  • Duplicate Payment: If an accidental double charge has occurred for the same product.
  • Incorrect Product Delivery: If the delivered product does not match the order placed.

To initiate a refund request, please email us at info@NexusSecure.shop with your order number and a detailed explanation of the issue.

3. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued under the following conditions:

  • You no longer want or need the software after delivery.
  • You purchased the wrong product but the correct product was delivered.
  • You fail to meet the system requirements necessary to run the software.
  • The license key has been activated or redeemed.

To prevent these situations, we encourage users to carefully review product descriptions and compatibility requirements before completing a purchase.
